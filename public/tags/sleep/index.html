The Importance of Sleep for a Healthy Lifestyle

Good sleep is one of the pillars of health and well-being. While we often focus on diet and exercise, sleep is just as crucial for physical and mental health. Here’s why sleep should be a priority in your daily routine.

Why Sleep is Essential

When you sleep, your body performs essential functions like cell repair, memory consolidation, and hormone regulation. Poor sleep can increase the risk of heart disease, obesity, diabetes, and even mental health disorders.
